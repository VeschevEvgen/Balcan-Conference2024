Project “CLEAR SKY”: An ecosystem for protecting cities

Participating countries

CLEAR SKY is an analog of the Iron Dome (Israel), which consists of drone technologies: fighter drones, drone pilots, drone detection radars, and other infrastructure.
For Ukraine, this is capital for the development and scaling of CLEAR SKY, advanced drone technology, international diplomatic support, and the possibility of selling CLEAR SKY to NATO countries under a franchise.
For Europe and other democratic countries, this is an opportunity to test the CLEAR SKY ecosystem in Kharkiv in real combat conditions, to get a ready-made solution (cheap, fast, high-quality) for the protection of European cities: R&D, Production, Infrastructure, Tactics of use.

JCI is facing a development crisis - new values are needed
Over the past 40 years, the JCI has shrunk by about 400,000 members and stands at 150,000 members. The decline is occurring at a rate of 10,000 members per year. If the situation is not changed, JCI may disappear in 15 years. At JCI's international events and at local levels, many participants recognize the crisis of values. Old members are leaving, new members do not really understand the value of JCI in the check and few are coming. We propose to introduce a clear and very interesting format for businessmen and investors - the JCI Investment Club. We will show how it works on the example of the Balkan Conference.
Investments - interest of Projects, Investors, Experts
Projects receive business funding and assistance in scaling. Investors get the best Projects for investment and professional support. Experts receive orders for their services and professional development.
Value for existing participants and attracting new ones
Value for existing members and attracting new members Existing JCI members from business and investor community will welcome the JCI Investment Club, and new members will join to gain experience. In practice, participants see value in participating in such professional Investment Clubs, for which they are willing to pay $200 per month!
Opportunity to create an investment franchise within JCI
Opportunity to create an investment franchise within JCI JCI Ukraine is ready to launch the JCI Investment Club and focus on HealthTech, as we have significant expertise in this area and have attracted over $700,000 in investments. Other JCI countries can take over other investment areas of the JCI Investment Club and turn them into a franchise, which will bring them and JCI additional monetization channels.
Ukraine is a country of investment opportunities
Ukraine is a country of investment opportunities Ukraine has a unique geographical position, the second largest territory in Europe, an educated, highly skilled population with European values and the ability to defend them, and economic assets that are many times cheaper than those in Europe. Ukraine is ready to develop, and the level of potential profits here is much higher than the level of risks.
War in Ukraine opens up markets
The war in Ukraine opens up markets Ukraine has become a testing ground for military and medical innovations, and it is possible to test things that are not possible in other countries. IT is very well developed in Ukraine, and in combination with HealthTech, it has already created many million-dollar businesses and is actively developing. Businesses, with relative similarity of parameters, go up in price from 2 to 5 times when leaving Ukraine for Eastern Europe, and from 5 to 10 times when entering the Central European, US, and Canadian markets. These are very interesting opportunities for Investors!
Ukraine-Estonia investment pooling
Ukraine-Estonia investment pooling Businesses entering the Estonian market increase their capitalization by 2 times or more. Businesses entering the Central European market through Estonia increase their capitalization by up to 5 times. The merger of JCI Ukraine and JCI Estonia will create a powerful synergy for investments in both countries and their scaling.
Transferring JCI Ukraine's experience to the Balkan countries
Transferring JCI Ukraine's experience to the Balkan countries After the Balkan Conference, we will transfer our investment experience to the Baltic Conference in September 2024 in Estonia and further to all JCI countries that request such experience. This will create value for them for existing members, attract new members and generate monetization for their local JCI and World JCI.
WealthTech platform Noosphere, JCI Ukraine
Noosphere WealthTech Platform, JCI Ukraine One of the organizers of BC is the WealthTech Platform Noosphere, which offers JCI World to automate the JCI Investment Club on its base by its Team. Noosphere has a billion-dollar capitalization potential and invites Investors to join its Team.

Other JCI Projects in Ukraine, Moldova, and the United States We have the first leaders of the competition for participation in BC, these are projects from different countries from JCI representatives. During May 2024, we will consider other projects on a competitive basis.

Security of the event
Attending an event in Uzhhorod is as safe as being in any other European country. Uzhhorod is located on the other side of the Carpathian Mountains from the rest of Ukraine. Nothing extraordinary happened in Uzhhorod during the entire war in Ukraine, which is why there is no curfew. Uzhhorod is protected by NATO air defense (Slovakia). The Balkan Conference will be attended by senior local government officials and will have a higher level of security.

Guarantees
Uzhhorod (Western Ukraine) is as safe as possible. But now the main team of the Balkan Conference organizers is at the forefront of the military clashes between Ukraine and Russia, in Kharkiv (Eastern Ukraine). Here, bombs fall daily, power is cut off, and so on. We are carrying out JCI's mission in the hottest spot in the world and defending the ideals of Freedom. This is our path. This involves various military risks. Accordingly, for reasons beyond our control, the Conference may be postponed for a certain period of time. We are already incurring expenses for organization. In the event of force majeure, we will refund 70% of your ticket payment and give you the maximum discount for the next event. If this happens, it will be your contribution to the freedom of Ukraine and support of JCI values.
Grants and donations

If you would like to help develop the format of the Balkan Investment Conference and the JCI Investment Club on its basis, please let us know!
